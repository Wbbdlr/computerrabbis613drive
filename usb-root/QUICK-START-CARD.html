Computer Rabbis

The Offline Torah Drive

תורה שלמה בכיס — בלי אינטרנט

How to open it

  1. Plug this drive into any Windows or Mac computer.
  2. Open it, the same way you'd open any drive or folder.
  3. Double-click the file named START-HERE.
  4. It opens right in your web browser. That's it.
Nothing to install. Nothing connects to the internet.
Safe to plug into any computer.

What's on it

  • Library — thousands of seforim: Tanach, Mishnah, Talmud Bavli & Yerushalmi, Rambam, Shulchan Aruch, Mishnah Berurah, midrash, mussar, chassidus, and more.
  • Zmanim & Luach — zmanim and Hebrew dates for anywhere on earth, any date, past or future.
  • Dictionaries — look up any Hebrew or Aramaic word across three dictionaries.
