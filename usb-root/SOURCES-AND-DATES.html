Sources & Data Dates

This drive was assembled on 2026-07-19.

Two kinds of content live here, and they age very differently:

  • Calculations (zmanim, Hebrew calendar, holidays, yahrzeits) — these are computed by formula on your own computer. They are correct for any year, past or future, and never go out of date. Updating the engines only adds features; it never fixes the dates.
  • Texts (the seforim library) — this is a snapshot taken on the date below. Later corrections or newly-added seforim on Sefaria/Dicta are not in this snapshot. To refresh, get a newer edition of this drive (see “Updates,” bottom).

Updates

This platform is published as an open project. The latest edition — a single folder you can copy onto any USB drive or SD card — is available at:

github.com/Wbbdlr/computerrabbis613drive

Because the calculations never expire, a drive never needs updating to keep telling you correct zmanim and dates. New editions mainly add seforim and refresh the text snapshot.
